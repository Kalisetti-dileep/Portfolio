About me

I am Dileep Kalisetti, a graduate student pursuing an M.S. in Data Science at Grand Valley State University with a strong academic background (CGPA 3.9/4.0) and a B.Tech in Computer Science. I have professional experience as a Data Analyst at Accenture and research experience as an AI Research Assistant at GVSU, where I worked on predictive modeling using crime data. My expertise spans programming in Python, R, SQL, and SAS, along with data visualization tools such as Power BI, Tableau, and Matplotlib. I have a proven track record of building impactful dashboards, automating reports, and developing machine learning models that drive data-informed decision-making. With certifications in Python and data processing, I bring strong analytical, technical, and problem-solving skills. I am passionate about leveraging data science to create meaningful insights and solutions, and I continuously seek opportunities to expand my knowledge and apply it in real-world projects.

Outside of academics, I enjoy fitness, blogging, and creative design, and my long-term goal is to become a data analyst who leverages AI and visualization to solve real-world problems.
